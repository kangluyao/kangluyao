### 👩‍🔬 About Me

I am currently a **Postdoctoral Researcher** at the *Institute of Botany, Chinese Academy of Sciences*.  
My research focuses on understanding how microbial communities regulate ecosystem processes under global change.

---

### 🎯 Research Interests

- 🦠🌍 **Microbial ecology & biogeochemistry**  
  Microbial community assembly and its implications for soil biogeochemical processes under global change  

- 🧬💻 **Integrative multi-omics & bioinformatics**  
  Applying multi-omics approaches and computational tools to unravel microbial ecological mechanisms
